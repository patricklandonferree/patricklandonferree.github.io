I'm a PhD student in philosophy of science at the University of Copenhagen. My work is based at Medical Museion (a medical history museum) and the Novo Nordisk Foundation Center for Basic Metabolic Research (CBMR). Prior to this, I double majored in chemistry and philosophy at UC San Diego, completed a PhD in cell biology at Duke University, and worked as a postdoc in exposure science at the Danish National Research Center for the Working Environment.
