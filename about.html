I'm a PhD student in the "Medicine, Culture, and Society" program at the University of Copenhagen, specializing in philosophy of science. My project examines the philosophical and sociological dimensions of single-cell biology, with a particular interest in the development of cell atlases.

Prior to this, I double majored in philosophy and chemistry at UC San Diego, completed a PhD in cell biology at Duke University, and worked as a postdoc in exposure science at a national lab in Denmark. For better or worse, I'm "interdisciplinary" through and through.
