Profile

  • I'm a PhD student based at Medical Museion, a medical history museum in the Department of Public Health at the University of Copenhagen. I'm also affiliated with the Center for Medical Science and Technology Studies (MeST) and the Novo Nordisk Foundation Center for Basic Metabolic Research (CBMR). My advisors are Karin Tybjerg and Sara Green.

    My project, tentatively titled Philosophy of Single-Cell Biology, draws on ethnographic fieldwork to examine scientific practices in single-cell and spatial biology, including the production and use of cell atlases.

    From March to mid-June, I'm a visiting researcher with Sabina Leonelli, Chair of Philosophy and History of Science and Technology at the Technical University of Munich.

Areas of Specialization
