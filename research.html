Overview

  • My research is situated in history and philosophy of the life sciences. I'm particularly interested in the role of technology and the implications that they have on our knowledge about biology and medicine. My PhD project, Philosophy of Single-Cell Biology, draws on ethnographic fieldwork to examine the epistemology of scientific practices in single-cell and spatial biology, including the production and use of cell atlases.
